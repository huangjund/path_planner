\mbox{[}m\mbox{]} --- The tie breaker breaks ties between nodes expanded in the same cell 

As the cost-\/so-\/far are bigger than the cost-\/to-\/come it is reasonbale to believe that the algorithm would prefer the predecessor rather than the successor. This would lead to the fact that the successor would never be placed and the the one cell could only expand one node.
